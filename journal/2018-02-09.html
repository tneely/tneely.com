Hiring Mediocrity: How Epic Targets the Unambitious to Succeed

Skilled labor becomes unskilled when there is a sufficient number of processes around it such that a layman, or group of laymen, may reproduce it.

Epic is Flat. There are no levels or official titles for most of the company hierarchy. Since Epic targets new college graduates, everyone starts as a Software Developer and stays that way throughout their tenure. A product lead is a Software Developer. A team lead is a Software Developer. A senior developer is a Software Developer. A junior developer is a Software Developer.
