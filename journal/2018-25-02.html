2018-25-02

With distractions, I don't have to think.

I don't have to feel,

or wonder,

or suffer,

or enjoy,

or be.

With distractions I am nothing.
And nothing is ok.

It's times like these, when the distractions are few,

when I have less than nothing

through which to pass the time,

that I remember to think,

and that I being to feel.

I don't like it.

Feeling.

It means facing my loneliness,

my inadequacies,

my self.

It is in these moments that I am best poised to take action,

and it is in these moments that I let myself down.

These words are all I leave to mark my moments of clarity.

Of insanity.

And when the ink dries, it will be time to move on.

To the next distraction.
